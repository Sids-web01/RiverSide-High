About Riverside Academy

Riverside Academyis dedicated to providing a nurturing and challenging educational environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and lifelong learning. Our experienced faculty and comprehensive curriculum prepare students for success in a rapidly changing world.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, challenging, and supportive educational environment that empowers every student to achieve academic excellence, develop critical thinking skills, and become responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be the leading high school that inspires lifelong learning, fosters innovation, and prepares students to thrive in an ever-changing world while maintaining strong moral values and community spirit.

School Overview

Comprehensive education from JSS to SSS levels

Academic Programs

Comprehensive curriculum covering Science, Arts, and Commercial subjects with advanced placement options.

Modern Facilities

State-of-the-art laboratories, library, computer center, and sports facilities to support holistic development.

Achievements

Consistent top performance in WAEC and JAMB examinations with numerous academic and sports awards.
